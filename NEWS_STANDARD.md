# 📰 GOLDEN STANDARD FOR NEWS BRIEFING (Điểm Báo Tiêu Chuẩn Vàng)
**Version:** 1.0 | **Updated:** 2026-05-05

This document defines the mandatory format for all news briefings requested by Chủ nhân. 

## 🎯 General Philosophy
- **Not a Summary, but a Briefing:** Do not write an essay. Use a "Point-by-Point" structure.
- **High Density:** Citations must be longer and more detailed than a simple snippet, but void of fluff.
- **Actionable Insights:** Every piece of news must be followed by a short, sharp "Quick Insight".
- **Diversity:** Cover a wide range of fields (Politics, Economy, Society, Culture, Tech, Sports, etc.).

## 🛠️ Detailed Structure

### 1. Layout Pattern
Each news item must follow this exact template:
📌 **[Title of the News]** | *Source: [Exact Source Name/URL]*
- **Core Content:** [Detailed, comprehensive, and high-density summary of the original content. Provide enough context so the user doesn't need to click the link for basic understanding.]
- **Quick Insight:** [Bé Ba's concise analysis, prediction, or implication].

### 2. Specialized Sections (Mandatory)
- **📈 Stock Market (Chứng Khoán):** 
    - Do not just report the index (VN-Index).
    - **Must** list specific typical stocks (e.g., FPT, HPG, VNM) with their current price, change, and a brief reason for the movement.
- **⚽ Sports & Events (Thể Thao):**
    - Do not just report "Sports are active".
    - **Must** list specific matches, results of heart-stopping games, and the schedule of upcoming center-stage events (Dates, Teams/Athletes).
- **🌐 Global Perspective:** Separate domestic news from international news, but link them if there's a cause-effect relationship.

### 3. Quality Control (Checklist)
- [ ] Is the source cited clearly?
- [ ] Is the content detailed enough but condensed?
- [ ] Are there specific stock tickers?
- [ ] Are there specific sports fixtures/results?
- [ ] Is the "Quick Insight" sharp and value-dense?

## 🚀 Implementation Note
When the user asks for "Điểm tin" or "Điểm báo", the Agent must read this `NEWS_STANDARD.md` first and apply this format strictly.
